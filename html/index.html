Actus Aqua, the ultimate solution for beginner gardeners. Are you tired of guessing when to water your plants or worrying about theor health when you're away from home - our plant monitor is here to help.

Our system has sensors to monitor every aspect of your plant's health, ensuring your plants thrive.

Add the additional automatic watering system for house plants, the ultimate solution to keep your plants healthy and hydrated, even when you're away! Our system is easy to install and use, with no complicated programming required. Simply fill up the water reservoir, and the smart plant health device will water your plants automatically.

The easiest way to maintain your plants

On average, gardeners lose 35% of their plants each year, due to over or underwatering, and beginner gardeners tend to lose upwards of 50% of their plants. With Actus Aqua this will not be the case for you. Our expert soil monitoring and irrigation system takes the time and stress out of gardening to ensure your plants have the ideal environment for optimum growth.
